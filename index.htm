About me

I'm a Ph.D. student of linguistics at UConn. Previously, I've studied linguistics and computer science in Vienna, Austria.

Broadly speaking, I work on syntax, morphology, and semantics, and I'm especially interested in Turkic languages and fieldwork on less-familiar languages.

Narrowly speaking, my work includes the following topics:

  • German impersonals that look like weird passives
  • the (non)-correlation of genitive case and possessive agreement in Turkic languages
  • before and after clauses and their semantic properties
  • the Turkish yes/no-question particle
  • tense and mood in conditionals

Please contact me if you are interested in the same topics as I am, or for any other reason!
